Drone Battery Insights

The battery is a crucial component in drones, as it powers all the essential systems including the motors, flight controller, sensors, GPS module, and communication interfaces. Most drones today use Lithium Polymer (Li-Po) batteries due to their lightweight nature and high discharge rates.

Wiggle in the Wind

PITCH

I want to move the drone forward. then I will slow down the front propeller speed while the rear propellers speed up. This will cause pitch motion

ROLL

rotation of the drone around its longitudinal axis, which runs from the front to the back of the drone,this movement is carried out by creating an imbalanced lift force

YAW

rotation of the drone around its vertical axis. When the drone turns left or right without changing its tilt (pitch or roll), that’s yaw movement.
